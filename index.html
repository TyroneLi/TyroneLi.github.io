Biography

I am now working at MeiTuan as a computer vision engineer, currently studying closely with Dr. Lin Ma and Dr. Zequn Jie, focuing on weakly/semi supervised 2D/3D detection and segmentation. Before that I obtained my B.Sc and M.Sc degree in Shenzhen University.

My research interests lie in detection and segmentation, especially in weakly/semi supervised learning.
